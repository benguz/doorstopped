Vaping

If vaping in the bathrooms prevents students from using the bathrooms, or it’s gross to see vape pods in toilets, the solution is making it cooler to poop than to vape.

At your next assembly or pep rally, or even at the beginning of a class or at lunch, say:

Raise your hand if you have ever not gone to the bathroom because people were vaping in there.

This will make it clear that most of the school is against vaping. 

Next: when someone leaves class to go to the bathroom, ask them, “Hey, are you going to vape?” 

Ask everyone. Even people who are obviously just going to the bathroom. When they get back, say, “Gone a long time there buddy, what did you get up to in there?”

Make it funny. Make it so, so annoying. Get people in all different friend groups to do it. This is funny shaming. You’re not narking on anyone, you’re not picking on anyone, you’re not giving them a chance to talk back, but you’re making it clearly lame to vape. 

If that doesn’t work, negotiate

Ask the people who vape what sucks most about school. What would they be willing to give up vaping in exchange for? 

Push past the non-answers like “there’s too much homework” and “let's make the school day shorter.” You’ll probably hear an answer you agree with.

Now you’re 10x more likely to change whatever their problem is because you have negotiating leverage. The vapers have committed to stopping if the change is made, and admin does not want people vaping. Get it done.

Learn to negotiate with admin and win here.
