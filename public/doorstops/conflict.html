Spicyyy 🔥🔥🔥

The tea 🍵
You hung out with the guy you like from chemistry class 🧪🧑‍🔬 this past Saturday. But 🧪🧑‍🔬and Gretchen were a thing a few weeks ago. On Sunday Gretchen angrily texted you because how could you hang out with 🧪🧑‍🔬 - it breaks girl code!!

You *don't* think you waaaaaaay broke girl code, you were gonna ask her for permission and just... didn't bring it up to her before the first hang out. You didn't even kiss!

The tea 🍵
One of your friends is flirting with your other friend’s ex. This other friend got super mad and now things are really tense.


🦈 Goal
Don't bite! What's gonna change her mind: yelling at her or listening to her side of the story and finding common ground?

🐢 Goal
This feels like a minefield. Maybe it feels like everything you say will be misinterpreted or turned against you. Diffuse the mines by:

  • Acknowledging how hard this is ("I'm anxious about how you'll read this")

  • Speaking slow ("From my perspective, it looks like...")

  • Asking questions (listening beats fighting!)

🧸 Goal
DONT try to patch things over at the expense of your own feelings. Honestly communicate how you feel!

♨️ Goal
Clearing the air from all that steam ya got boiling! If you're worried whatcha say'll be misinterpreted, try:

  • Acknowledging how hard this is ("I'm anxious about how you'll read this")

  • Speaking slow ("From my perspective, it looks like...")

  • Asking questions (listening beats fighting!)

🌉 Goal
Be honest with how their drifting apart is making YOU feel without starting drama!
