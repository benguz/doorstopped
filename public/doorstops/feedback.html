Anonymously email a teacher with feedback

A fix.school email address will email your teacher with subject line: "Anonymous student reaching out with feedback"

We have a template you can copy paste! Also: we never see or save any information about you.

Your teacher's email:

Recommended Template:

Hi [Teacher's Name],

Thank you so much for the time you've put into making [Class Name] wonderful. I was hoping to share some feedback about parts of class I've been struggling with. I have huge respect for what you do as a teacher and am giving feedback anonymously because I feel anxious about sharing.

[Here, explain your specific concerns or frustrations. For example: "I've noticed that there's a lot of focus on memorizing facts rather than understanding the processes."]

[Suggest a solution. For example: "I understand that memorization can be important, but I personally feel that I'd learn more effectively if we could incorporate more opportunities for hands-on or experiential learning."]

Thank you for listening. [Optional: I feel that the majority of my peers would agree, and those who don't would be open to finding a compromise if we had a chance to discuss as a class.]

Regards,
Anonymous [High School Name] Student

Email body:

In addition to what you write, the email will end with the following footer text: "This email was sent by doorstopped.org, an education nonprofit building student-teacher trust. If you feel our anonymous feedback tool was misused, please let us know."
