Best option: send your teacher an email before the school year starts.

Ask them to send out a pronounciation survey to every student in the class

Subject: My name's hard to pronounce, can you

Dear [Teacher],

My name is [Your Name], and I'll be one of your students this year!

My name is hard to pronounce, and it's always stressful to correct a teacher's pronounciation on the first day.

Would you be open to sending out a name pronounciation survey to the whole class - I think it would make everyone feel more comfortable. You could use google forms or voiceform.

[optional two sentences if you're comfortable with them: If not, that's totally fine! Here's a short voice recording on how to pronounce my name.]

Thanks so much,
[Your first and last name]

If you go by different pronouns than your peers, this is a great place to mention that, or clarify what names to call you in front of your parents vs. with your friends. You can send an anonymous email asking for a pronoun survey here or just add a sentence to this email if you're comfortable.
