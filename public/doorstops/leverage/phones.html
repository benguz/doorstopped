Phones = Leverage to fix school

"Less homework! Shorter school days! Better food!" Inundated with these revolutionary ideas from students, schools closed their suggestion boxes a long time ago. But with phones in your hand and between 2.4 and 5 GHz of bandwidth at your fingertips, you have leverage. Instead of constantly fighting teachers over phone use (pain, angst, sadness), ask them, what do you want in exchange?

Not sure what's wrong with class, or don't think there's anything you could change? Check out this page for strict teachers and this page for nice ones, and this page for an example of changing curriculum and bad policy.
